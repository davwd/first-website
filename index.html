An MOV varistor, short for metal oxide varistor, is a vital component for safeguarding electronic devices from harmful voltage spikes. Made from zinc oxide and other metal oxides, it acts like a voltage-dependent resistor. Under normal voltage conditions, it offers high resistance, allowing minimal current to flow. However, when a voltage surge hits, the MOV varistor switches into action. It dramatically reduces its resistance, diverting excess current away from sensitive electronics and safely grounding it. This shields your devices from potential damage caused by lightning strikes, power outages, or other transient voltage fluctuations.
